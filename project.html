Projects

E-Blood Bank Management System

My project focused on developing an e-blood bank management system. This system facilitates donor registration, patient enrollment, and efficient inventory management for blood banks. Donors can schedule appointments, while urgent patients can locate the nearest available donors for timely transfusions. This platform streamlines blood donation processes, ensuring optimal blood supply management and quick access to blood for those in critical need.

Voice Assisted GMail

This project focused on creating a voice-based Gmail interface specifically designed for individuals with visual impairments. This innovative system enables users to perform all email-related functions using voice commands, eliminating the need for traditional keyboard and mouse interactions. From composing and sending emails to managing inbox notifications and accessing attachments, every aspect of Gmail functionality is seamlessly integrated into the voice interface. This technology aims to enhance accessibility and empower individuals with visual impairments to independently and efficiently manage their email communication using natural language commands.
